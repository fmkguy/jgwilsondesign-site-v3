About Me

Ever since I could hold a crayon, I’ve scribbled, colored, and made things. Today, I scribble and color less, and illustrate, design, and code more.

Traveler of Places

When I was a kid, my dad was in the US Air Force. That means I had the huge privilege of getting to travel the country. I haven’t done any traveling outside the US, though, except when I was born in Germany while my parents were stationed there.

I’ve always had an eye for design and detail, and that’s a huge part of what makes me the successful developer and designer that I am today. Surprisingly, it all happened by accident. An entire decade ago, I wanted a website to bring people together to help me work on an animation project I was doing while in high school, so I built it. The animation never really got anywhere, but I learned enough about web development to land my first job. Since that day 10 years ago, I’ve been using, refining, and growing those skills, continuing to learn and keep pace with the rapidly-changing web and its technologies.
